About

Glimmers And Musings

This is the blog of a programmer, a nerd and a writer. By sheer dumb luck, these three happen to be the same person.

Purpose

I want to improve my development skills, and running my own blog seems to be a good way to go about it. I've chosen to write in Markdown, and transitioned from Jekyll to Cryogen so I could dig under the hood more. (I'm principally a Clojure dev)

Content

I'll be posting creative writing and RPG stuff, software nerdery with an emphasis on Clojure, Godot Engine, and Linux.
